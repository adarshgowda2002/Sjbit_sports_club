About Us

SJB Institute of Technology is an educational institution started by His Divine Soul Jagadguru Padmabhushan Dr. Sri Sri Sri Balagangadharanatha Mahaswamiji, Founder President of Sri Adichunchanagiri Shikshana Trust Â® in the year 2001. The aim of this institution has been to impart quality education in a conducive environment. This becomes incomplete without all-round development in sports and cultural activity. The Institution has created excellent facilities in sports and provided opportunities for students to become good sports person and inculcate good sports man spirit among students. SJBIT always encourages sports in a celebrated way. The department of physical education and sports has been active since its inception in promoting physical activities and sports. It has always played a key role in organizing sports activities and tournaments. It has excellent state of the art infrastructure for indoor sports like 3- wooden surfaced Badminton courts, Table Tennis, Carrom, Chess, Wrestling etc., and outdoor games like Cricket, Volleyball, Kho Kho, Handball, Basketball etc., A well-structured sports committee is constituted comprising of faculty members representing each branch headed by the Principal and coordinated by Director of Physical Education. Inter-department competitions were held across all branches of the Institution for both indoor and outdoor sports events encouraging students to take part in it. Coaching camps were conducted for the students selected to the teams representing college at university and state levels. Facilities such as Transportation, Boarding, and Lodging, Sports uniform and medical benefits were provided to the students representing college at various levels of sports activities. Staff sport events are held in both indoor and outdoor activities. Well established gymnasium and yoga center is made available to all the students, faculty and staff. A gym instructor is available to train the students and staff of the institution. This has contributed in SJBIT being ranked among top ten Institutions in VTU sports and cultural activities, SJBIT has won rupees 1 lakh cash award from VTU, three times through its excellent performance in sports activities. Sports department organizes coaching camps in association with external resource persons every year. With all these supports from the management many students brought laurels to our institution.
